The aim of Triangulum Sport as a company is to provide the platform & setup the eco-system where game developer can develop the online games for Esports Company.
Considering the current trend and development in Esports, it has become a real sport not the entertainment merely. There is huge potential in this arena, principally in India.
This platform would be available for Gamers, Game Developers, team owners and business stakeholders which would bring all on single platform to work and earn altogether.
Focusing on providing platform for organizing events and broadcasting video streaming via online channels like YouTube, Twitch.tv etc.
