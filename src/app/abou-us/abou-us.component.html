What we Are -

The aim of Tringulum Sport as a company is to provide the platform & setup the eco-system where game developer can develop the online games for Esports Company. Considering the current trend and development in Esports, it has become a real sport not the entertainment merely. There is huge potential in this arena, principally in India. This platform would be available for Gamers, Game Developers, team owners and business stakeholders which would bring all on single platform to work and earn altogether. Focusing on providing platform for organizing events and broadcasting video streaming via online channels like YouTube, Twitch.tv etc.

Bringing various stakeholders i.e. Gamers, Game Developers, Broadcasters etc. on a single platform to work together to take this industry to the new heights. Also it would be organizing the grand events like real sports events where gamers would be competitive as an individual as well as team. It would be evolved as new job creating industry by enabling trainings, investment and promotion. It will continue to scale in terms of both popularity among players, fans and revenue for businesses.
