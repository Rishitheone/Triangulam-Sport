E-sports -

E-sports is turning into a religion with more than 809 million people actively involved, and the numbers are expected to reach 1.59 billion people in the coming years. Remember the times when your mom used to shout at you for playing video games throughout the day? Well to be honest now even mom are playing them. It has levelled up from a mere mode of entertainment to a new era profession. Competitive video game playing is so huge that it garnered over 258 million unique views both online and in person, as compared to 204 million views raked in by NFL. E-sports is raging and it’s here to stay, maybe you should join the bandwagon with us!
