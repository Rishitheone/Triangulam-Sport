Anshuman Mishra

Anshuman Mishra (Advisor)

Mr. Mishra, an investment banker by profession, is the founder of MPH holdings. He has worked with most of the giants in the banking industry. He has even lent his expertise in strategic international relationships to prestigious law firms in the US, including Procopio, Akin Gump & Mayer Brown in LA. Mr. Mishra is also a chief mentor of NY based Business Council for International Understanding and also senior strategy advisor to India’s top corporates. His vast knowledge and unique perspective to business has even compelled the President of the United States, and other world leaders to invite him on their tours to India. Mr. Mishra vehemently believes in the huge potential that India has to offer. He has seen the worldwide growth in popularity of e-sports, and is sure that the same tsunami is soon going to hit India too.

Keith Bradshaw

Keith Bradshaw (Advisor)

If you’ve heard of Adelaide Oval, known as one of the most picturesque and iconic venues in Australia if not the World, then you most probably know Keith Bradshaw too. He is the CEO of South Australia Cricket Association (SACA). A Chartered Accountant in his days, Keith has also represented Tasmania in cricket. Keith has been actively following his passion for cricket, though behind the screen. He is Chief Executive of the South Australian Cricket Association and also former Secretary and Chief Executive of the Marylebone Cricket Club at The Mecca of Cricket - Lord’s Cricket Ground in London. Bradshaw is highly regarded in England for his stewardship of MCC as chief executive The Lord’s, and has earned a similarly lofty reputation with the SACA at Adelaide Oval. His passion for sports has made him excited about Triangulum. He feels that sportsmanship is of highest value whether it’s on-field or on-line.

Ravi Bopara

Ravi Bopara (Advisor)

Ravinder Singh "Ravi" Bopara an English cricketer who plays for Essex and England in all three formats. Originally a top-order batsman, his developing medium pace bowling has made him an all-rounder and he has the best bowling figures for England in a Twenty20 International. Bopara has also played for Karachi Kings in the Pakistan Super League, Kings XI Punjab in the Indian Premier League, Sydney Sixers in the Big Bash League and Chittagong Vikings in the Bangladesh Premier League.

Bopara played for the team Karachi Kings in Pakistan Super League (PSL) held at U.A.E in February 2016. The captaincy for the Karachi Kings was handed down to him after Shoaib Malik stepped down from the role. His team finished fourth overall. He won the player of the series award for the PSL 2016 by making 329 runs and taking 11 wickets within 9 matches for the Karachi Kings.

In September 2018, he was named in Balkh's squad in the first edition of the Afghanistan Premier League tournament. In July 2019, he was selected to play for the Glasgow Giants in the inaugural edition of the Euro T20 Slam cricket tournament. However, the following month the tournament was cancelled.
