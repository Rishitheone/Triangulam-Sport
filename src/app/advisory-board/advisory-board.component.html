Anshuman Mishra (Advisor)

Mr. Mishra founder MPH Holdings (Pvt offices of India’s leading families) is an investment banker by profession, he has worked for top global banks. He works closely with some of the world’s largest sovereign wealth funds. He has served on the Capital Markets teams of globally iconic financial institutions & has been instrumental in creating one of the most successful aircraft finance business & a business advisory company in India.

Mr. Mishra worked for some of the most prestigious law firms in the U.S., including Procopio, Akin Gump & Mayer Brown in Los Angeles, San Diego, Chicago, New York, Washington, DC & Paris offices. He enjoys excellent relationships with the legal fraternity both in the US & in India. Most of his legal work involves developing strategic International partnerships, procuring finance arrangements & advising on M&A.

Mr. Mishra's financial expertise transcends borders & cultures. Having studied under Personal tutelage of Transcendental Meditation Teacher Maharishi (known as the guru to the Beatles), he brings a unique perspective to Business, he has been invited by world leaders including U.S. President on their tours to India.

Mr. Mishra holds a Bachelors Degree in Economics & a Master of Laws from prestigious U.S. institutions. Mr. Mishra is chief mentor of NY based Business Council for International Understanding. He is Senior Strategy Advisor to India’s top corporates. He considers Asia’s two leading business leaders as his mentor - Chairman, Reliance industries & Tata Group. He enjoys extremely close relations with Mahindra, Aditya Birla Group, Saudi Aramco/Abu Dhabi National oil company & World’s largest Tech Fund - Softbank founder - Masayoshi San. Mr Mishra, maintains friendly relations with Carlos Slim Family, Arnault Family & senior Executives at Disney.

Keith Bradshaw

Keith Bradshaw (Advisor)

Keith Bradshaw Keith Bradshaw was appointed as the Chief Executive of the South Australian Cricket Association in January 2012 at Adelaide Oval. He was formerly Secretary and Chief Executive of the Marylebone Cricket Club at Lord’s Cricket Ground in London. Keith became the fourteenth Secretary and Chief Executive of the Marylebone Cricket Club when he took up the position in October 2006. He was the first MCC Secretary to hail from overseas, having played first-class cricket for Tasmania before embarking on a distinguished business career. Keith retired from first-class cricket in 1988, to complete his studies at the University of Tasmania (where he graduated in Commerce) and pursue his commercial career as a chartered accountant, which included spells with both Price Waterhouse and Deloitte, where he was a Partner before joining the MCC.

Whilst Chief Executive of the MCC Bradshaw had a place on the administrative board of the England and Wales Cricket Board and it has been reported that he had some say in the removal from office of England Head Coach Duncan Fletcher OBE and former India Head Coach in April 2007.

Ravi Bopara

Ravi Bopara (Advisor)

Ravinder Singh "Ravi" Bopara an English cricketer who plays for Essex and England in all three formats. Originally a top-order batsman, his developing medium pace bowling has made him an all-rounder and he has the best bowling figures for England in a Twenty20 International. Bopara has also played for Karachi Kings in the Pakistan Super League, Kings XI Punjab in the Indian Premier League, Sydney Sixers in the Big Bash League and Chittagong Vikings in the Bangladesh Premier League.

Bopara played for the team Karachi Kings in Pakistan Super League (PSL) held at U.A.E in February 2016. The captaincy for the Karachi Kings was handed down to him after Shoaib Malik stepped down from the role. His team finished fourth overall. He won the player of the series award for the PSL 2016 by making 329 runs and taking 11 wickets within 9 matches for the Karachi Kings.

In September 2018, he was named in Balkh's squad in the first edition of the Afghanistan Premier League tournament. In July 2019, he was selected to play for the Glasgow Giants in the inaugural edition of the Euro T20 Slam cricket tournament. However, the following month the tournament was cancelled.
