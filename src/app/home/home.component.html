What is e-Sports

E-sports is a form of organized competitive gaming between professional gamers and could be defined as a combined entity of gaming and sports both in teams and individually, compete against each other in different computer. Video games are not only medium of entertainment, it has been grown as a new era profession where players can work as professional and earn money. Esports has been forecasted to have the potential to be bigger than any sport that has ever been played.


Esports is a booming global industry where skilled video gamers play competitively in the same way that traditional sports have competitions in baseball, basketball, and football. Esports encompasses competitions across a variety of video games and this industry is real, growing globally, and investable. In fact, over 380 million people watch Esports worldwide both online and in person. More people watched the 2016 world finals of popular Esports game League of Legends (43 million viewers) than the NBA Finals Game 7 that year (31 million viewers). With its fragmented landscape and digital platform, the Esports sector holds promise for a multitude of monetization opportunities. – an activity that has shown a phenomenal growth in interest the past years. Due to its globalized and online nature, viewership for Esports is significant, making it comparable to traditional sports


Triangulum Sports – Galaxy Of Gamers

The Aim of Triangulum Sports-

The aim of Triangulum Sports as a company is to provide the platform & setup the eco-system where game developer can develop the online games for Esports Company. Considering the current trend and development in Esports, it has become a real sport not the entertainment merely.

There is huge potential in this arena, principally in India. This platform would be available for Gamers, Game Developers, team owners and business stakeholders which would bring all on single platform to work and earn altogether.

Focusing on providing platform for organizing events and broadcasting video streaming via online channels like YouTube, Twitch.tv etc.

Bringing Various Stakeholders-

Bringing various stakeholders i.e. Gamers, Game Developers, Broadcasters etc. on a single platform to work together to take this industry to the new heights.

Also it would be organizing the grand events like real sports events where gamers would be competitive as an individual as well as team.

It would be evolved as new job creating industry by enabling trainings, investment and promotion. It will continue to scale in terms of both popularity among players, fans and revenue for businesses.
